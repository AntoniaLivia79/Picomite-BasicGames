DOG STAR ADVENTURE
==================
By Lance Micklus (1979)

SCENARIO
--------
You are a secret agent aboard
General Doom's Battle Cruiser.
Princess Leya needs your help!
Infiltrate the ship, collect
vital intelligence and items,
then return to your space ship
and blast off to safety.

Your score depends on how many
valuable items you bring back
to your ship's storage bay.

COMMANDS
--------
Type two-word commands:

  GO NORTH  or just  N
  GO SOUTH         or S
  GO EAST          or E
  GO WEST          or W
  GO UP            or U
  GO DOWN          or D
  LOOK             or L
  INVENTORY        or I
  HELP             or H

Other commands:
  GET [item]    DROP [item]
  SHOOT [target]
  SAY [word]
  READ [item]
  EAT [item]
  FEED [target]
  OPEN [item]
  PRESS BUTTON
  SHOW [item]
  SCORE
  SAVE [filename]
  LOAD [filename]
  QUIT

CARRYING LIMIT
--------------
You can carry up to 6 items.
Drop items in your ship's
storage compartment (Room 1)
to free up space.

GUARDS
------
After 25 turns, armed guards
may appear at any time!
When a guard appears you must:

  SHOOT GUARD

You need your BLASTER for this.
Without a blaster or ammo
you will be captured.

YOUR SHIP
---------
You begin in the COCKPIT (Room 2).
Your STORAGE COMPARTMENT is
below in Room 1. Leave via the
exit to explore the Cruiser.

KEY PUZZLES
-----------
GETTING STARTED
  In the SUPPLY DEPOT get a
  BLASTER and AMMUNITION.

STRATEGY PLANNING ROOM
  Beware: a GUARD and SCIENTIST
  are here. SHOOT the GUARD
  first, then SHOOT SCIENTIST.
  Collect the COMMUNICATOR,
  KEYS and DEATH RAY SCHEMATIC.

TRACTOR BEAM
  Find the tractor beam control
  room and PRESS BUTTON to
  turn the tractor beam OFF.

FLIGHT DECK DOORS
  READ the GRAFFITI in the
  bathroom for a useful clue.
  Carry your COMMUNICATOR then
  SAY SECURITY to open the
  flight deck doors.
  WARNING: if you are on the
  flight deck when doors open
  there is no air - you die!

COMPUTER ROOM
  Use CSAVE TAPE to copy
  secret ATTACK PLANS onto
  a cassette tape.

TESTING LAB
  SHOOT the SCIENTIST to safely
  collect the TURBOENCABULATOR,
  CLOAKING DEVICE and I.D. CARD.

ATTACK ROBOT
  A ROBOT guards the security
  desk. Feed it a HAMBURGER to
  destroy it and clear the way.
  (Hamburger must still be warm!)

THE JAIL
  Use your KEYS to OPEN DOOR
  and free PRINCESS LEYA.

THE VAULT
  Show your I.D. CARD to pass
  security and enter the vault.
  Collect the MALIDIUM CRYSTALS
  - the most valuable treasure!

LAUNCHING
---------
To blast off you need all of
the following in Room 1:
  - Anti-matter FUEL
  - TURBOENCABULATOR
And all of these to be done:
  - Tractor beam turned OFF
  - Flight deck doors OPEN

Then go to the COCKPIT (Room 2)
and PRESS BUTTON to launch!

SCORING
-------
Score = valuable items stored
in your ship (Room 1):

  Anti-matter Fuel      5 pts
  Shinestone Necklace  20 pts
  Map of the ship      20 pts
  Princess Leya's Cape  5 pts
  Secret Attack Plans  20 pts
  Death Ray Schematic  20 pts
  Cloaking Device      20 pts
  Micro Laser Gun      20 pts
  Turboencabulator      5 pts
  Malidium Crystals    30 pts
  Princess Leya        50 pts

  MAXIMUM SCORE: 215 points

WIN CONDITION
-------------
Launch your ship with as many
scored items as possible.
A perfect game scores 215 pts!

TIPS
----
Get BLASTER and AMMO first.
Read the GRAFFITI for clues.
Shoot enemies before collecting
  nearby items.
Drop items in Room 1 regularly
  to stay under carry limit.
Use HELP in any room for hints.
Save your game often with SAVE.
