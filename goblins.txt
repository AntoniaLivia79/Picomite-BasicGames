GOBLINS
=======

SCENARIO
--------
You are an adventurer exploring a
100-cell underground dungeon.
Find the TREASURE hidden within,
then reach the EXIT to escape!

THE DUNGEON VIEW
----------------
The screen shows 21 cells centred
on your position. Cells outside
the dungeon are shown as X.

  @  = You
  .  = Empty space
  l  = Loot
  k  = Key
  d  = Door
  g  = Goblin
  T  = TREASURE
  E  = EXIT
  X  = Wall / out of bounds

Status bar below the view shows:
  loot:X  keys:X  TREASURE:yes/no

CONTROLS
--------
  a  Move left
  d  Move right
  q  Quit

ITEMS AND ENCOUNTERS
--------------------
LOOT (l)
  Walk onto loot to pick it up.
  Your loot count increases by 1.
  Goblins will steal one piece
  to let you pass.

KEY (k)
  Walk onto a key to pick it up.
  Your key count increases by 1.
  Keys unlock locked Doors.
  One key is used per door.

DOOR (d)
  Requires a key to pass.
  You cannot pass without a key.

GOBLIN (g)
  Goblins block your path.
  If you carry loot, the goblin
  steals one piece and lets
  you pass.
  With no loot, you are blocked.

TREASURE (T)
  Walk onto the Treasure to
  claim it. Status bar will
  show TREASURE: YES.
  You MUST collect the Treasure
  before the Exit will open.

EXIT (E)
  The way out of the dungeon.
  Sealed until you have the
  Treasure. Walk onto it to WIN!

WIN CONDITION
-------------
Collect the TREASURE (T) then
reach the EXIT (E).

TIPS
----
Collect loot early to help get
  past goblins later.
Gather keys before you need them
  so you are not stuck at doors.
The dungeon is 100 cells wide -
  explore both directions!
